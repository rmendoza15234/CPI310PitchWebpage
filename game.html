Game Concept:
Puzzle/Hazard, Side-Scrolling Platformer with Co-Op functionality(non-network, local machine)
Score(based on survival time)
Difficulty(from object hazards)
Location/Design(clouds, floating islands)
Co-Op(one player has the power of fire, the other the power of water. Alternatively, if one player; the player can switch between the two players. Puzzle/hazard platformer)
Storyboarding:
Both of the players are creations of the overseer god, Moltonui, whose physical form is that of molten rock and mossy stone,
and constantly chases the player/s across the playing field.
Moltonui is unhappy with his creations, and seeks to be able to remedy his mistakes.
The goal of Moltonui is to harness the living being’s energy, to reform them into his perfect vision.
The player/s’ ultimate goal is to reach the All-stone, locked away deep in the caverns of Moltonui’s resting place, which contains the power to life;
but could this be their undoing? Their alternative is equally as bleak.
